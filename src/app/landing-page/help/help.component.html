Promo Tourism Privacy and Policy

Last Updated: 3 January 2024

  • Account Creation: Merchants must provide valid business information and contact details.

  • Approval by Ministry: Registration is subject to approval by the Ministry of Tourism, based on compliance with regulations.

  • Account Security: Merchants are responsible for securing their account credentials and any activities under their account.

  • Updates and Accuracy: Regular updates and accuracy of account information are required.

  • Product Listing Requirements: Listings must meet specified quality standards and be relevant to tourism. This includes ensuring that all descriptions and images accurately represent the product and comply with legal and ethical standards.

  • Editing and Updating Products: Merchants have the right and responsibility to edit and update their product listings to ensure accuracy and currency. Changes should be made promptly to reflect any alterations in the product, pricing, or availability.

  • Removal of Products: Merchants can remove listings from the platform, provided they give a specified notice period. They must also honor any bookings or purchases made prior to the removal of the listing, ensuring customer satisfaction and trust.

  • Purchase Procedure: Clear guidelines on how customers can make purchases, including available payment methods, terms of sale, and procedures for order processing and confirmation. This aims to ensure a transparent and user-friendly purchasing experience for all customers.

  • Receipt Generation: Automatic generation of receipts and booking confirmations upon successful transaction. This ensures customers have a record of their purchases and provides necessary details for any follow-up or customer service needs.

  • Review Policy: Customers are encouraged to leave reviews of their experiences with products or services. All reviews are subject to moderation to ensure fairness, appropriateness, and compliance with platform guidelines. This policy is designed to maintain the integrity and reliability of customer feedback.

  • Approval Criteria: Specifies the detailed criteria used by the Ministry for the approval of merchant accounts. This includes assessment of business legitimacy, adherence to tourism and commercial standards, and compliance with legal requirements.

  • Rejection and Appeals: Outlines the process for handling rejections of merchant account applications. It also provides a mechanism for merchants to appeal against rejections, detailing the steps for submitting an appeal and the review process for appeals.

  • Data Collection: Provides clarity on the types of data collected from users, including personal, transactional, and usage data. It details the purpose of data collection, such as improving user experience, providing customer support, or complying with legal obligations.

  • Privacy Protection: Describes the measures in place to ensure the protection and security of user data. This includes the use of encryption, secure data storage practices, and regular audits to prevent unauthorized access and data breaches.

  • Consent for Data Use: Emphasizes the requirement of explicit consent from users for the collection, use, and sharing of their data. Users must be informed about how their data will be used and given the choice to opt-in or opt-out of data collection practices.

  • Liability Limitations: Outlines the limitations of the system's liability in the event of disputes or issues arising from the use of the service. This includes disclaimers for indirect losses, data inaccuracies, and service interruptions.

  • Resolution Mechanisms: Describes the procedures for resolving disputes between users (merchants and customers) and the platform. This may involve mediation, arbitration, or legal processes, depending on the nature and severity of the dispute.

  • Notification of Amendments: Details the procedures for notifying users about changes to the terms and conditions. This includes the methods of communication (e.g., email, platform notifications) and the timeframe in which users will be notified before changes take effect.

  • Acceptance of Changes: Explains that continued use of the platform after the implementation of changes constitutes acceptance of the new terms. Users who do not agree to the new terms are advised to cease using the platform.
